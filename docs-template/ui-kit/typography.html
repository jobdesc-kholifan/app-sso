Body & Lead Text

Make a paragraph stand out by adding .lead or styling standard <p> tags.

Lead Paragraph: Vivamus sagittis lacus vel augue laoreet rutrum faucibus dolor auctor. Duis mollis, est non commodo luctus.

Standard Paragraph: Curabitur blandit tempus porttitor. Nullam quis risus eget urna mollis ornare vel eu leo. Maecenas sed diam eget risus varius blandit sit amet non magna.

Small Text: Cum sociis natoque penatibus et magnis dis parturient montes, nascetur ridiculus mus.

Muted Small Caps: Done c id elit non mi porta gravida at eget metus.

Semantic Text Colors

Convey meaning through color with a handful of emphasis utility classes.

.text-primary - Primary action text color.

.text-success - Success indicators and messages.

.text-danger - Alerts, errors, and destructive actions.

.text-warning - Cautions and warning indicators.

.text-info - Neutral information or tips.

.text-muted - De-emphasized secondary text.

Color Utilities
